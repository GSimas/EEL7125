Detailed Description

A Study that performs an Inverse Kinematics analysis with a given model.

Inverse kinematics is the solution of internal coordinates that poses the model such that the landmark locations (markers) and/or body rotations (as measured by IMUs) affixed to the model minimize the weighted least- squares error with observations of analogous markers and IMU orientations in their spatial coordinates. Observations of coordinates can also be included.

Ajay Seth
